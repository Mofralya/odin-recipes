Spaghetti

Description

A classic Italian pasta dish featuring a savory tomato sauce and perfectly boiled noodles.

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti noodles
  • 1jar of Marinara sauce

Steps

  • Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the noondles until tender.
  • Drain the water and toss the noodles with warmed marinara sauce.
