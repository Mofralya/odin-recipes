Tacos

Description

The quick and easy tacos are perfect for a busy weeknight and can be customized with all your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 pack of Taco seasoning

Steps

  • Brown the ground beef in a skillet and drain the excess fat.
  • Add the taco seasoning and a splash of water, then simmer for 5 minutes.
