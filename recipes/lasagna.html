Lasagna

Description

A classic Italian dish with layers of pasta, meat, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles.
  • Ground beef.
  • Ricotta cheese.

Steps

  • Boil the noodles.
  • Cook the meat sauce.
  • Layer and bake.
